My Journey

I am a robotics engineering studen driven by the challenge of deploying software that goes out and interacts with the real world.

My interest in robotics started with a fascination for autonomous systems—how a combination of sensors and code can navigate a complex environment. Currently, I focus on mobile manipulation and perception-action loops, specifically looking at how robots can automate repetitive tasks in lab environments.

I’m currently pursuing a Master's Degree at Carnegie Mellon University with a focus on Summer 2026 opportunities where I can apply my skills in ROS2, Python, and control systems to solve real-world problems.
